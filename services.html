Extensions


Activated when relevant. Scoped separately.

All extensions are optional growth levers designed to accelerate outcomes beyond the core retainer. A core retainer is mandatory for all add-ons.

01

Digital Experience & Website Strategy

Create a clear, brand-aligned digital home that supports discovery, credibility, and long-term brand recall. Includes website narrative, content structure, copy direction, and visual guidance.

02

Paid Media & Growth

Accelerate visibility and reach for specific campaigns, launches, or growth objectives through strategic paid media planning, creative direction, and performance optimisation.

03

Creator & Community Marketing

Build authentic visibility through creators and communities aligned with the brand's culture. Includes creator strategy, influencer shortlisting, and collaboration concepts.

04

Launch Strategy & PR Visibility

Introduce new menus, spaces, campaigns, or collaborations with clarity and impact. Covers launch narrative, campaign planning, PR angle development, and media coordination.

05

Event Amplification & Documentation

Extend the life and impact of on-ground events beyond the moment. Pre-event storytelling, on-ground direction, documentation, and post-event recap strategy.

06

Brand Systems, Packaging & Merchandise

Translate the brand into physical touchpoints that reinforce identity and recall. Visual system design, menu design direction, and production-ready asset handover.

07

Thought Leadership & Founder Visibility Strategy

Position founders or leadership as credible cultural voices, not promoters. Includes founder narrative, content themes, and visibility strategy across platforms.

Each extension runs independently with its own scope, timelines, milestones, and outputs.
A core retainer is mandatory for all add-ons.
